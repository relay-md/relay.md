How it works

The power of Markdown, Frontmatter and Obsidian

Log-in

With your Account, you can register on relay.md with just one click. Other identity providers may be supported in the future.

Access Key

Obtain your custom access key from your relay.md profile. Use the Access key for authentication against our API.

API Access

We provide API access on api.relay.md to upload your markdown files via standard HTTP requests.

Obsidian Plugin

Use the power of Relay.md fully integrating into Obsidian via a plugin. Get newly shared content and post new documents with a single click.

Sharing

Use the frontmatter key relay-to to list recipients individually or entire teams including a topic.
